History

X leadtheread.com was born out of neccesity. Somehow despite millions of websites with millions of different niches, no website has yet fully tackled the common problem of forgetting a books title but remembering the plot. Often the solution is a barbaric approach of asking people on subreddits dedicated to the problem. No More!
The algorithm used is a key word weighted vector search between the user query and plot summaries found via multiple API's.
I plan to continually upgrade the algorithm and eventually implement movie, podcast, and quote search engines.
